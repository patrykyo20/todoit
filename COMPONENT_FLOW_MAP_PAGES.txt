#### **3.1 Strona główna (/loggedin)**
```
app/loggedin/page.tsx
    ↓
TaskList (components/layout/Task/TaskList.tsx)
    ↓
Skeleton | TaskSort | Tasks | AddTaskWrapper | Tasks | CompletedTasks
    ↓
Task (components/layout/Task/Task.tsx) [dla każdego taska w Tasks]
```

#### **3.2 Strona Today (/loggedin/today)**
```
app/loggedin/today/page.tsx
    ↓
SkeletonToday | TitleView | Tasks (Overdue) | Tasks (Today)
    ↓                    ↓
TaskSort (w TitleView)  Task (components/layout/Task/Task.tsx) [dla każdego taska]
```

#### **3.3 Strona Calendar (/loggedin/calendar)**
